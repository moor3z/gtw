The G.T.W Way

Six standards behind every garden we touch

Standard 01

Clear

Brambles, overgrowth, old sheds and rubbish — full or partial clearances stripped back to a blank canvas. Ideal for house sales, probate and end-of-tenancy.

Standard 02

Cut

Overgrown lawn rescues and regular cuts through the season. Edges strimmed, hedges shaped, shrubs pruned — finished properly, every time.

Standard 03

Trim

Hedges reduced and shaped, shrubs and small trees pruned hard or taken out entirely — crisp lines, not a quick once-over.

Standard 04

Remove

Everything we cut leaves with us on the day. Every load goes to a licensed site with full paperwork — never a lay-by.

Standard 05

Tidy

Paths swept, surfaces cleared, gates closed behind us. The only sign we were there is the garden itself.

Standard 06

Maintain

Once it's back under control, we'll keep it that way — fortnightly or monthly visits so your garden never gets away from you again.
