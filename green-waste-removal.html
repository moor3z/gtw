Common Questions

Green waste removal — FAQs

What counts as green waste?

Garden material — grass cuttings, hedge and shrub trimmings, brambles, weeds, leaves, branches and small roots. We don't take general household rubbish, rubble, soil or building waste.

Do I need to bag the waste up first?

No need. Bagged, piled or loose is all fine — we'll load it however it is. If it's easier for you to bag it that's welcome too, but it's not required.

Where does the green waste go?

Every load goes to a licensed site for proper disposal — never fly-tipped, never a lay-by. Most green waste is composted or recycled rather than sent to landfill.

How much does green waste removal cost?

It depends on the volume — a few bags is very different from a trailer-load. Send a photo of the pile and you'll get a fixed price, usually within one working day. The price we agree is the price you pay.

Do you do the cutting too, or only removal?

Both. This page is about removing waste you've already created, but if you'd rather we did the cutting back as well, that's a garden clearance — just tell us and we'll quote for the lot.
